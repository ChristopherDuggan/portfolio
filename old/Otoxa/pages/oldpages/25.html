Earlier today I finished a book called Brave New World by Aldous Huxley. What made it stand out when compared to the other pre-WW2 dystopian sci-fi stories is it's about a utopia.

The society the book describes is one that prioritizes happiness and stability and succeeds in securing them through ruthless disregard for all else. Like Nineteen Eighty-Four or Farenheit 451, it pretty well predicts some of the less desirable aspects of society we've stumbled ass-backward into. Plus there's a communal butt slapping so what's not to like?+
