February 19, 2018

The air is thick with fear, anger, and anxiety. There is uncertainty about the future of a kind that I've never experienced in my lifetime. It can seem like you are all alone in a world that's gone mad and you are powerless to affect it.

But the world hasn't gone mad. It's the people in the world, people who feel as alone and powerless and anxious and afraid as you do. The only remedy is friendship and love, spread indiscriminately and without reservation. Do what you can.

Next update comes Thursday.

Be kind.

-Chris
